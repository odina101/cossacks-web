After the death of Jan Sobieski in 1696, Frederick Augustus became King of Poland with the support of the Russian and Saxon armies. The Polish gentry demanded he return the lands captured by Sweden. In 1700 the Saxon army allied with Russia entered into the war against Sweden. In this campaign, our enemy is to be taken very seriously. The Swedish army is one of the best in Europe. Therefore, the offensive campaign should be preceded by in-depth preparation.
