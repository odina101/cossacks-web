Orthodox peasants in Ukraine are being oppressed by the Polish authorities. The Poles have also infringed on the freedom of the cossacks, dismantling the register cossack formations and sending them to work for their magnates. In the long run, hetman Kalinovsky plans to disarm the Zaporizhian Sich, the Orthodox bulwark in Ukraine, as well. Your objective is to forestall the punitive operation of the Polish troops and protect the native lands from pillage.
