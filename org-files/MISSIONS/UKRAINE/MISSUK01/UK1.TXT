1629
Polish authorities often recruited Cossacks. All who served in the Polish army were included in a so-called register. Cossacks not registered by the Polish government took refuge in Zaporizhzhya. There were many fugitive serfs there. Tatar and Turkish raids proved most harmful to Christian settlements. You are a kuren ataman and people expect you to conduct decisive actions. Erecting a paling and organizing the maintenance of your kuren are your top priorities.
