In this mission you will have to take up arms under the banner of Great Britain. 
There is an ally on the opposite side of the island. 
To the north of your island lie trade routes of the English (orange). There are also two islands rich in resources in the north. These are controlled by pirates.
The goal of the mission is to defeat the nation of the King of Spain's nephew (blue). 
The blue player is on the continent to the east of your island. Spanish merchant ships supply the enemy with gold. Try to neutralise them.
The continent is controlled by the hostile French.
