The 18th century. Military operations will take place on the islands. 
The allied Austria and France are at war with the alliance of Poland, Saxony and Bavaria. 
You are in command of the Austrian army on the central island (see map). Polish and Saxon troops are also located on this island and they will attack you from different sides. Bavaria has captured the island in the north-east and will attack you from the sea. 
You and your ally must defeat the enemy alliance.
