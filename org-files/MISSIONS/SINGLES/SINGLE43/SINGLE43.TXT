Owing to its advantageous geographical location, at the point where trade routes crossed, the Saxon principality was one of the richest German states. 
As a young officer, you are faced with excellent opportunity of promotion in both rank and wealth. Your objective is to form a company, build a barracks and provide supplies.  
With a big squad of soldiers and constructors, you will head for the frontier area. There you must build a small military settlement: a mill, town hall, dwellings, mines for procuring of gold, metal and nitre. Your workers will sow fields and begin collecting resources. Also, you will need to chop wood and quarry stone in order to construct a barracks. 
When your financial state reaches the required level, it will be possible to begin recruiting additional soldiers.
