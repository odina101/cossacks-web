This mission won't be an easy one. You will play for Ukraine, the rich and peace-loving country famous for its craftsmen, songs and hospitality. Your Swedish allies have their own agenda in this war ...
Poland, Russia and Turkey are your enemies. Their alliance is a serious power combining skill, courage and endurance.
Military operations will take place on land. 
To defeat them you must be very careful with the tactics you apply on the battlefield. Defeat your enemies and become a strong and wealthy state!
