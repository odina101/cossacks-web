In 1741 the war for Austrian succession broke out. The rivals of Austria were the allied Prussia, Spain, France and Sweden and its allies were England, the Netherlands and later Russia. The enemies contended for a part of the Austrian domain. After Emperor Karl had died, his daughter Maria Theresa inherited the country since there was no male heir. Frederick suddenly invaded Silesia and occupied it. Your task is to leave the Neisse Fortress and block the way of the Prussian troops. The Prussian infantry and artillery have you greatly outnumbered.
