Twilight descended on Europe, deadly war had shattered the world. The lands were strewn with thousands of corpses, scorched towns and plundered monasteries. The Thirty Years' War caused the world immense losses and turned Europe into a battlefield. Austria happened to be at the heart of the catastrophe, its cause and effect, one of the sides in that terrible opposition of European states, and the leading contender for dominance in Europe. You are to make your way through numerous battles of the 17th and 18th centuries and try to rescue your country from devastation and conquest. Under your command will be many well-trained soldiers, but your adversary will be strong and crafty too, which is why it will not be easy to accomplish this mission.
