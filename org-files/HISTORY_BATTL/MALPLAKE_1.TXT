In this battle the allied army of Austria, England and Holland (red) has to conduct a continuous assault and cause maximum damage to the French military forces (blue). The allies have to annihilate 637 French units.
In order to win, the French forces (blue) should hold the defensive for a certain time with minimum losses (keeping an army of at least 371 soldiers) or partly destroy the English army. The French will have to destroy 999 English units.
The side that destroys part of its enemy's forces wins the battle.
If the French are not attacked by the alliance, they will be considered winners after a certain amount of time has passed.
When half of the specified number of units are destroyed, the players will be informed of their losses.
