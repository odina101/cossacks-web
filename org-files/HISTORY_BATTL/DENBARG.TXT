In order to win this battle, the English army (red) must either capture and hold any three of the five specified positions or entirely destroy the Scottish forces (blue). "Capture" means all enemy military forces must be cleared from the area and your soldiers must take up positions there instead.
The Scottish army will win if they destroy the enemy entirely or manage to hold their positions for a certain amount of time.

Press the CTRL key to see the locations that must be fought for.
