Summer, 1660
You have survived many battles and long voyages, and your scouts have found a place that will be ideal for building your colony. The island of Laretia is large and rich, and will allow you to build a comfortable place to settle permanently.
News from Europe suggest that you not only made many enemies, but friends also. Many people take with sympathy to your cause, and see your undertaking as a way to escape from their own troubles in the Old World...
